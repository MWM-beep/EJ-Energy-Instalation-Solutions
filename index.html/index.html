About Us

EJ Energy Solutions was born out of a passion for powering homes and businesses with safe, reliable, and sustainable energy. What started as a small team of electricians in Nairobi has grown into a trusted solutions provider for electrical, solar, and security systems across Kenya.

Over the years, we’ve built our reputation on one thing: putting our clients first. Whether it’s a simple home wiring project, a full-scale solar installation, or advanced security systems, we treat every job with the same level of care, professionalism, and attention to detail.

Our people are at the heart of what we do. From engineers and technicians to customer support, we’re a team driven by innovation and committed to making energy solutions smarter, safer, and more affordable.

Why Choose EJ Energy Solutions?

  • Tailored solutions designed to fit your exact needs
  • Reliable installations backed by skilled and certified experts
  • Commitment to safety, sustainability, and long-term value
  • Responsive support and after-sales service you can count on

Looking ahead, we’re excited to continue shaping the future of energy around the world from embracing green technologies to expanding smart home and EV charging solutions. Our journey is just beginning, and we’re here to power yours.

Company Profile

EJ Energy Solutions

EJ Energy Solutions is a fully registered and licensed company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We provide end-to-end energy and electrical solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Our portfolio spans electrical installations, solar power systems, lighting, security solutions, generator backup systems, smart home technology, and EV charging stations.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, reliable, and energy-efficient solutions while upholding the highest standards of safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become the region’s leading provider of electrical and energy solutions, recognized for quality, innovation, and service excellence.

Our Core Values

  • Quality & Excellence
  • Integrity & Professionalism
  • Innovation & Continuous Improvement
  • Customer Focus & Reliability
  • Sustainability & Safety

Our Services

  • Electrical Installations (Domestic, Commercial & Industrial)
  • Solar Power Systems (Off-grid, Hybrid & Grid-tied)
  • Security Solutions (CCTV, Alarms, Access Control, Electric Fencing)
  • Backup Generator Systems
  • Smart Home Automation
  • EV Charging Stations
  • Preventive & Emergency Maintenance

Certifications & Compliance

We operate in compliance with ERC, EPRA, IEC, and KEBS standards. Our engineers and technicians are fully certified and trained to deliver safe, reliable, and efficient installations that meet local and international requirements.

Our Commitment

At EJ Energy Solutions, we are committed to excellence. Every project we undertake is guided by a promise to deliver sustainable, cost-effective, and customer-focused solutions that stand the test of time.
